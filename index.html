The Buildradiator

Purpose

The Buildradiator is a webapplication that gives you a handy overview of your builds

Vision

We strongly believe that a development team will be more productive and involved when everything surrounding the project is visible and transparant. This includes domainmodels, architecture, relations with external actors, organisation of the team, etc.

One of things that helps achieving this are digital infoscreens that are tailored to the project needs. However some of these parts can be generalized.

Mission

Most teams that are using continuous integration servers (Jenkings, TeamCity, Travis CI,...) are interested in their builds. We try to give you an overview, by providing a webapplication that summarizes those builds. You can integrate this application in your infoscreen

Practical

For now, due to limited resources, we only support Teamcity. The project however is open source (MIT licence), so yourself can easily extend or modify it according to your needs.

The application is accessible through the following url: app/#failed/{hostname}(replace hostname by stub, to see fake data)

Don't forget to configure your buildserver to allow requests from buildradiator.com (support teamcity)

You must also turn on logging in as a guest user

This is still work in progress. So be aware of the fact that breaking changes are possible
